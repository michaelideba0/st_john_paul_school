About St John Paul II Secondary School

St John Paul II Secondary School is proudly known as the cheapest and most affordable school in Ebonyi State, providing high‑quality education to every child regardless of background. Our mission is to form disciplined, intelligent and morally upright future leaders.

The school is spiritually guided and managed by our esteemed Spiritual Director, REV. FR. KENNETH NOMEH (NSO NA AGHA), whose dedication to academic excellence and moral formation shapes every aspect of our learning environment.

With committed teachers, strong discipline, a peaceful learning environment and various academic and extracurricular programs, St John Paul II stands as a citadel of excellence across Ebonyi State.

Enrollment Requirements

Our School Facilities

St John Paul II Secondary School provides modern and well‑equipped facilities to support excellent learning.

Library

Our library is stocked with a wide collection of academic textbooks, reference materials, literature and research resources to help students study effectively.

ICT Laboratory

The school has a functional ICT lab equipped with computers and internet access to help students develop digital skills and computer literacy.

Science Laboratory

Our science lab is fully equipped for Physics, Chemistry and Biology practicals, giving students hands‑on scientific experience.
